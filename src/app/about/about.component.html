Techonology township

Techsol Engineering was established in the year 1998 as an engineering company to provide services in the electrical work sectors. From providing services, it soon focused its activities into Turn Key solutions. From the year 2000, Techsol's range of services expanded to Design, Erection, Procurement, and Execution.

Vision & Mission

In the year 2012, Techsol Engineering was incorporated in the Joint Stock of Companies of Bangladesh and upgraded to the status of Limited Company from Proprietorship. Since 2001, Techsol has also been engaged with the manufacturing of Transformers & Switchgears of different ranges from its leased premises in Dhaka. The vast prospects and with the growth of the Company, Techsol's recent achievement features acquisition of land and building a state of art factory floor measuring around 48500 square feet at Salna, Gazipur under Gazipur City Corporation. Our corporate office is situated at BDBL Bhaban (Level-19), 12 Karwan Bazar C/A, Dhaka-1215, Bangladesh.
